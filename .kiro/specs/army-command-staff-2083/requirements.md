### **NEW BUSINESS MODEL - Chapter-Based Purchases** 🆕

**Professional Approach**:
- **Individual Chapter Purchases**: Pay-per-module system (NPR 3,000-5,000 per chapter)
- **Sequential Progression**: Must complete all lessons in current chapter to unlock next
- **Bundle Offers**: Complete course packages with discounts
- **Strict Completion Requirements**: No skipping ahead - maintains learning integrity

**Monetization Strategy**:
```
FREE Introduction Module (Always Accessible):
- Module 0: Introduction, Vision, Mission & Course Overview

Total if bought separately: NPR 20,000

Standard Bundle (Most Popular):
- All 5 Chapters - NPR 18,000 (Save NPR 2,000)
- Includes: All chapters, 3 mock exams, certificate, study schedule

Premium Bundle (Best Value):
- All 5 Chapters + Premium Features - NPR 25,000 (Save NPR 7,000)
- Includes: All chapters, 5 mock exams, certificate, mentor access,
```

**Professional Features**:
- **Lesson-Level Progression**: Must complete each lesson sequentially within a chapter
- **Chapter Unlock Logic**: 
  - Individual purchase: Chapter locked until purchased

- **Completion Criteria**: Watch videos + pass quizzes (70%+) + submit assignments
- **Progress Verification**: System validates lesson completion before unlocking next
- **Achievement System**: Badges for lesson/chapter completion
- **Certificate**: Only for bundle buyers who complete all 5 chapters

  - Administration in War & Peace
  - Training
  - Leadership & Man Management
  - Organization of Nepali Army
  - Military Act & Laws
  - Logistic System

#### Module 2: Contemporary Affairs (100 marks)
- **Weight Distribution**: 40% National, 30% Regional, 20% International, 10% Military Tech
- **Focus**: Current events with military/security relevance
- **Content Types**: Daily updates, weekly revision, essay practice

#### Module 3: Military History & Strategic Thoughts (100 marks)
- **Topics**:
  - Military History (battles, campaigns, wars)
  - Strategic Thinkers (Clausewitz, Jomini, Napoleon, Slim, Prithivi Narayan Shah)

- **Focus**: Tactical problem-solving
- **Content Types**: 
  - Appreciation methodology lessons
  - Practice exercises (PRACTICE mode)
  - Submission reviews (FEEDBACK mode)
- **Assessment**: Written appreciation and plans

#### Module 5: Lecturette (100 marks)
- **Focus**: Oral presentation skills
- **Content Types**:
  - Presentation technique lessons
  - Practice lecturettes (FEEDBACK mode)
  - Video/audio submissions
- **Duration**: 10-15 minute presentations

- **Goal**: Deepen knowledge and apply concepts

  - 10-minute presentations
  - Case studies

### Phase 3: Consolidation (Month 5-6)
- **Goal**: Exam readiness and mastery
- **Milestone**: Mock Exam II & III
- **Content**:
  - Full appreciation exercises
  - Formal lecturettes

  - Targeted revision
  - Faculty review sessions

- **Color Coding**:

  - Yellow: 5-10 minutes remaining
  - Red: <5 minutes remaining

  - 5 minutes: "5 minutes remaining" toast notification
  - 1 minute: "1 minute remaining - submit soon" alert
- **Auto-Submit**: When timer reaches 0:00, automatically submit quiz
- **No Extension**: Timer cannot be paused or extended
